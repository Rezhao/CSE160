Virtual World

Notes to Grader: I implemented a maze game/story to the world where the end goal is to help this party panda find their birthday cake. Use WASD to move and QE or mouse cursor to rotate the camera. I based the world off of an actual panda's usual habitat by filling it with bamboo and a dirt ground. I wanted to make it seem like you were lost in a bamboo forest. I also mapped out the maze to include dead ends and multiple paths to try and trick the player from reaching the end easily.

Please use a browser that supports Canvas
