Party Panda

Notes to Grader: I added animations for all legs and head. The head bobs up and down while the legs move in three parts: thighs, calves, and feet which can all be controlled separately through the sliders. I used a cone shape for the panda's party hat. When you click and drag on the canvas, it rotates the panda in that direction. For poke, I made it so when you hold down shift and click, the panda goes into happy mode and when shift is released, the panda goes back to regular. Happy mode is where the panda has smiley eyes and a rainbow arc around it's head. It also sways side to side like it's wagging it's tail. I also tried optimizing the performance by editing how often the buffer gets created in Triangle.js and showed the performance on the screen.

Please use a browser that supports Canvas
